The Basics

Key terms for digital citizens:

The technical reality that once data is online, it is stored on multiple servers and archives, making "deletion" nearly impossible.

Active: Information you share on purpose (posts, photos).
Passive: Information left behind without you knowing (cookies, browsing history, GPS metadata).

Safety Guidelines

  • Privacy Check: Perform a monthly audit of your app permissions.
  • The "Public" Mindset: Assume everything you type is being recorded by the platform, even in private DMs.
  • Positive Curation: Build a footprint that helps you—post your projects, certificates, and interests.

Busting Myths

The Myth: "Snapchat/Stories are temporary."

The Reality: Third-party apps, screen recordings, and server logs mean no content is truly ephemeral.
